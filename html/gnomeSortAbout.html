Gnome Sort

What is gnome sort?

Gnome sort is a sorting algorithm. It operates by finding the first position where two elements are out of order and swapping them. This process repeats until the items are fully sorted.

How did it get the name "gnome sort"?

A computer scientist observed that the algorithm mimics how a gnome would sort flower pots. Because the gnome cannot see over any of the pots, he can only deal with the two on either side of him in his current position.

Does gnome sort have any advantages?

Glad you asked! Actually, it does. Because the gnome can only deal with his current position, he does not have to remember any other positions. The space complexity of gnome sort is merely O(1).

True. But it probably takes forever.

Yes it does. Gnome sort has a time complexity of O(n^2). For this reason, it also goes by the name "stupid sort".
